Do's & Don'ts


Do’s



  • 1)Do ask your patient's permission to visit before you arrive.

  • 2)Do wash your hands and sanitize them before you touch the patient or hand the patient something you've been touching.

  • 3)Do turn off your cell phone, or at least turn the ringer off.

  • 4)Do leave the room if the doctor or provider arrives to examine or talk to the patient


Dont’s




  • 1)Don't enter the hospital if you have any symptoms that could be contagious.

  • 2)Don’t stay with the patient for a long time. Keep the visit short.

  • 3)Don't bring outside food/fruits inside hospital for patients/patient's attendants. The hospital serves well balanced vegetarian meals.

  • 4)Do not smoke; consume alcohol and non-vegetarian food in the hospital premises.

  • 5)Don't bring flowers, bouquets for the patient.

  • 6)Don't attempt photography or video shoot in the hospital premises.
